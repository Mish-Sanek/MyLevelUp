The Company and the Client are hereinafter referred to individually as the “Party” and collectively as the “Parties”.

2. Scope of Training

2.1 The Company provides the Client with online training on the topic “Data Analytics” and/or “Digital Marketing” and/or “Web Development”.

3. Deadline

3.1 This training is carried out for a period of no more than 12 months.

4. Eligibility criteria

4.1 To qualify for the money-back guarantee, the Customer must meet the following criteria:

  • 4.1.1 Be at least eighteen (18) years of age;
  • 4.1.2 Must be proficient in spoken and written English;
  • 4.1.3 Must be a citizen of the Philippines and legally authorized to work in the Philippines; And
  • 4.1.4 The client must pay the full cost of training.

5. Tariff

5.1 The cost of training is determined by the Company in the form of an invoice issued to the Client from time to time (“Cost”), which may be subject to an additional discount at the absolute discretion of the Company.

5.2 Payment for tuition can be made either in full or in three (3), six (6), nine (9) or twelve (12) equal monthly payments. In the case of the latter option, the installment plan provider may charge interest rates depending on the installment plan agreed between the Client and the provider.

5.3 The client is not entitled to training/qualifying job offer/money back guarantee if he/she has not made full payment of the fee. If the Client selects the installment plan option, any delay or failure to pay even one installment will result in termination of the Client's training program/Qualifying Employment Offer/Money Back Guarantee.

6. Money back guarantee

6.1 The Company provides a full guarantee of the return of the Remuneration paid by the Client, for the avoidance of doubt, at any time the amount to be returned to the Client does not include any applicable taxes, fees, surcharges and bank commissions associated with the calculation of the Remuneration (hereinafter referred to as the “Money Back Guarantee”), provided that the Client has not received a Qualifying Job Offer, and only in the following circumstances:

  • 6.1.1 The Client has successfully completed training with the Company and passed all (100%) required assignments through the online platform within the official deadlines provided by the Company to the Client for the entire training period determined by the Company from time to time (but not exceeding 12 months), with a grade not less than 80% for each of the project assignments for the entire period of study and not less than 80% for the final project.
  • 6.1.2 The Client has complied with all the Company’s requirements, including, but not limited to:
  • a) Successful completion of all tasks assigned by the Company or its support team within three (3) months after completion of training, but no later than December 15, 2023.
  • b) Personally submit 300 job applications on job search sites (including, but not limited to, Indeed) within 60 days of completing training, but no later than December 15, 2023.
  • c) Providing answers to any tasks on job search sites within 3 (three) working days.
  • d) appearing for an interview with the hiring partner. If the Client fails to attend the interview, Refocus will have every right to refuse any other opportunity for an interview.
  • e) demonstrate a "good faith effort" (as determined in the Company's sole discretion) to actively seek employment to obtain a Qualified Job Offer (defined below) within six (6) months of completion of training;
  • f) Customer must respond to communications from the Company, including, but not limited to, responding to all Help Desk messages and other communications from the Company, in each case within three (3) business days;
  • h) The Client must provide all correspondence with potential employers indicating the inability to obtain a Qualified Job Offer.
  • 6.1.3 Provided that if at any time prior to completion of the course the Client is able to obtain employment with or without Refocus's guidance, assistance or efforts, the money back guarantee will not apply and the process described in this Section 6 will be deemed completed and fully utilized .

6.2 As stated in paragraph 6.1.2(c) above, a Qualified Job Offer is an offer of work that meets any of the following conditions:

  • 6.2.1 For the position:
    subject to the Client completing the Data Analytics course - in the field of Data Analytics;
    subject to the Client completing the Digital Marketing course - in the field of Digital Marketing;
    subject to the Client completing the “Web Development” course - in the field of web development;
  • 6.2.2 As an employee, apprentice, trainee or independent contractor;
  • 6.2.3 Paid, including in cash;
  • 6.2.4 It is expected that the average work hours will be 30 hours or more per week or several offers of part-time work equivalent to an average work hours of 30 hours or more per week; And
  • 6.2.5 The duration of the project is expected to be at least 4 weeks.

6.3 The Company reserves the right, at its sole discretion, to change / cancel / adjust / supplement the Money Back Guarantee.

7. Refund of Comission

7.1 The Company is not obliged to return the remuneration to the Client in case of non-compliance with any of the terms of this Agreement, in particular, clauses 6.1 and 6.2.

7.2 In addition, the Company does not return the Client’s remuneration in the following cases:

  • 7.2.1 The Client was unable to successfully complete the training;
  • 7.2.2 Client has not made a good faith effort to obtain a Qualified Job Offer; or
  • 7.2.3 The Client intentionally sabotages a Qualifying Job Offer.
  • 7.2.4 Client rejected due to Client's prior conduct, misconduct or discriminatory record.
  • 7.2.5 The client applies for a money-back guarantee due to fraud.

7.3 If the Client believes that he is entitled to receive a Money Back Guarantee, he should email info@refocus.me. The letter must indicate the following:

  • 7.3.1 Full name of the Client and his contact information (address, telephone, email);
  • 7.3.2 Confirmation that the Client has successfully completed training, completed all job search requirements (and any other applicable requirements) and complied with all conditions set out in this Agreement;
  • 7.3.3 Confirmation that, in accordance with clause 6.1.2, the Client was unable to receive a qualified job offer within 6 (six) months from the date of successful completion of training by the Client at the Company; And
  • 7.3.4 Therefore, the Client is entitled to a full refund of the Remuneration, as applicable, in accordance with the terms of this Agreement.

7.4 The Company reviews the request for a refund of the Reward and may request additional information or verification regarding the Client, which the Client must provide within 5 days of the Company’s request. If the Company believes that the Client’s request is genuine and that the Client has fulfilled all the criteria specified in clauses 6.1 and 6.2 of this Agreement, then the Company, within the period agreed upon between the Parties, returns the entire amount of the Remuneration to the Client.

8. Indenpendence

8.1 The Client shall indemnify and keep fully indemnified the Company and its associates/agents/subagents/employees/directors/representatives for all liabilities, costs (including legal fees on an indemnity basis), expenses, damages and losses, including any direct, indirect or consequential damages , loss of profits, loss of goodwill and all interest, penalties and other reasonable costs and expenses incurred by the Company and/or its affiliate(s)/agent(s)/subagent(s)/employee(s)/director(s)/representative (s) as a result of any breach by the Client of this Agreement or as a result of a breach of any other agreement(s) between the Parties, as well as as a result of the actions or inactions of the Client or any associate(s) / agent(s) / subagent(s) / employee(s) s) or representative(s).agent(s)/employee(s)/director(s)/representative(s) of the Client in violation of any Applicable Law or custom or trade practice.

8.2 This provision shall survive termination of this Agreement.

This Agreement constitutes the entire Agreement between the Parties and supersedes and replaces all previous discussions/correspondence and acts/agreements, memorandums of understanding, if any, between the Parties with respect to the matters covered by this Agreement, whether written, oral or implied. ;
